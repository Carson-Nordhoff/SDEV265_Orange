About MTG

MTG offers an experience of wonderous immersion with endless possibilities that requires players to use strategy and creativty to excel in the game. Magic: The Gathering, also known as "Magic" or "MTG", is a collectible card game first published by Wizards of the Coast in 1993. Since then it has grown to be one of the most popular trading card games in the world. With a dedicated fan base, MTG capitvates millions of people with its rich lore and intricate gameplay.
